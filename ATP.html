How to understand the draws?
ATP##: The number after the ATP is the number of points awarded to the winner
In the draws next to the player's name you can find a number or initials
The most common are:
#: Lists the seeded players
WC: Wild Card, entered the tournament by an invitation
PR: Protected Ranking, player ranking before an extended injury
LL: Lucky Looser, player that lost during qualifying session but entered the main draw
